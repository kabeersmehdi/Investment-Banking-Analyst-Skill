## ADJUSTMENT FACTORS

### Upward (+0.25x to +1.5x each)
- Recurring revenue > 70%
- Contracts > 12 months
- Management team in place
- Revenue growing 15%+ YoY
- EBITDA margin > 20%
- Proprietary IP / brand
- Roll-up platform potential
- Clean / audited financials

### Downward (-0.25x to -1.5x each)
- Single customer > 25% revenue
- Owner = primary salesperson
- Revenue declining YoY
- EBITDA margin < 10%
- Tax returns only
- High customer concentration (top 3 > 60%)
- Lease < 18 months
- Commodity market / no moat

## REVENUE QUALITY SCORING

| Revenue Type | Score |
|-------------|-------|
| Contracted recurring (auto-renew) | 10/10 |
| Subscription / retainer | 9/10 |
| Repeat non-contracted | 7/10 |
| Repeat project-based (RFP) | 5/10 |
| New project-based | 3/10 |
| One-time / transactional | 2/10 |

Blended Score = Σ(Revenue Type % × Score)
8.0–10.0 → Premium multiple | 6.0–7.9 → Mid-range
4.0–5.9 → Discounted | <4.0 → Significant discount

## MULTI-SERVICE BUSINESSES
Revenue-weight the multiple across service lines.
Add +0.25x to +0.50x diversification premium.

## ASKING PRICE
- Set at bull case or 10–15% above base
- Leave 5–15% negotiation room
- Never below base case
- Check SBA 7(a) cap ($5M)
- Flag if aggressive assumptions required
