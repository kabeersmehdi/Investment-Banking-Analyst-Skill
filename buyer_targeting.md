## BUYER PERSONA FRAMEWORK

### Persona 1: Individual Buyer / Owner-Operator

**Profile:**
- First-time buyer or second business purchase
- Corporate professional, industry veteran, or entrepreneurial exec
- Net worth: $500K–$3M
- SBA financing likely (10% down on qualified deals)

**Best Fit When:**
- SDE < $1.5M
- Business manageable by a capable operator
- SBA eligible (real assets, positive cash flow history)
- Operations not highly technical or specialized

**Key Concerns:**
1. "Can I run this without the current owner?"
2. "Will the SBA approve the loan?"
3. "What's my Day 1 cash flow after debt service?"
4. "What happens if I lose the top customer?"

**Outreach Angle:**
"Proven, profitable business with [X] years of operating history and
a clear transition plan. SBA-eligible with as little as 10% down.
The seller is committed to a [X]-month handoff."

**Where to Find:**
- BizBuySell.com buyer inquiries
- BizQuest.com
- Local SBA lender referral networks
- LinkedIn (search: "looking to acquire business" + industry)
- EO / Vistage / YPO networks
- Business school alumni networks

---

### Persona 2: Search Fund / ETA Buyer

**Profile:**
- MBA or senior executive searching full-time for 1–2 years
- Self-funded or investor-backed ($500K–$2M search capital)
- Sophisticated financial buyer with operator mindset
- Deal size target: $1M–$6M EV

**Best Fit When:**
- SDE > $750K (meaningful enough to justify search economics)
- Clear growth levers exist
- Business has recurring or contracted revenue
- Some management depth below the owner

**Key Concerns:**
1. "Is there a defensible moat here?"
2. "Can I grow EBITDA 15–20% annually?"
3. "Is management retainable?"
4. "Can I get institutional financing (SBA or non-SBA)?"

**Outreach Angle:**
"This platform offers a rare combination of stable cash flows,
demonstrated demand, and tangible growth opportunities in a
fragmented market ripe for consolidation."

**Where to Find:**
🌐 [Search live for current ETA networks]
- Search Fund Primer (Stanford GSB) alumni list
- Search Fund Coalition
- ETA (Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition) LinkedIn groups
- SMBash conference attendees
- Acquisition Lab community
- MBA program ETA clubs (HBS, Wharton, Booth, Kellogg)
- Twitter/X: #searchfund #ETA community

---

### Persona 3: Strategic / Corporate Acquirer

**Profile:**
- Existing operator in same or adjacent industry
- Seeking geographic expansion, customer acquisition, or capability
- Can pay synergy premium (0.5x–2.0x above financial buyer)
- Often moves faster than PE with less process friction

**Best Fit When:**
- Target has unique customers, geography, or capabilities
- Customer overlap is limited (accretive, not redundant)
- Integration path is clear and simple
- Revenue concentration less concerning (they absorb customers)

**Key Concerns:**
1. "Does this extend our footprint / capability?"
2. "Are key employees retainable post-close?"
3. "What is the integration cost and timeline?"
4. "What's the customer overlap / cannibalization risk?"

**Outreach Angle:**
"This acquisition provides immediate access to [X customers /
geography / capability] with an established team and minimal
integration complexity. Synergies are clear and quantifiable."

**Where to Find:**
🌐 [Search live for strategic acquirers in the industry]
- LinkedIn: VP Corp Dev, Head of M&A, CEO — filter by industry
- Mergr.com (recent acquirer database)
- Industry trade associations (member directories)
- Recent press releases: "[INDUSTRY] company acquires"
- EDGAR filings for public company roll-up strategies

---

### Persona 4: Financial Buyer (PE / Family Office / HoldCo)

**Profile:**
- Institutional capital with 3–7 year hold horizon
- Requires management team (cannot be owner-dependent)
- Seeks platform + add-on roll-up opportunity
- Deal size: $2M+ EBITDA (typically)

**Best Fit When:**
- EBITDA > $2M
- Management team in place (not owner-run)
- Fragmented market with consolidation opportunity
- Recurring / contracted revenue base
- Clear path to value creation (multiple expansion + growth)

**Key Concerns:**
1. "What is the EBITDA quality and sustainability?"
2. "Is there a management team that survives owner exit?"
3. "What's the organic growth CAGR?"
4. "What is the realistic exit multiple in year 5?"
5. "Are there add-on targets in this market?"

**Outreach Angle:**
"This platform offers a compelling entry point in a fragmented
[industry] market with [X]% EBITDA margins, a tenured management
team, and multiple organic + inorganic growth vectors.
Exit multiple expansion is supported by market consolidation trends."

**Where to Find:**
🌐 [Search live for PE firms active in this industry]
- Axial.net (post deal for buyer matches)
- PitchBook / Preqin (PE fund database)
- Mergr.com (recent acquisitions by PE)
- LinkedIn: "[INDUSTRY] private equity" + Principal / Partner
- Industry conference sponsor lists
- GF Data buyer network
