## AUTO-DETECTION FROM DEAL CONTEXT
After /start, automatically flag risks based on answers:

| If Context Shows | Auto-Flag |
|-----------------|-----------|
| Top customer > 25% | ⚠️ Customer concentration — HIGH |
| Top 3 > 60% | ⚠️ Revenue concentration — HIGH |
| Owner does sales | ⚠️ Owner-dependent revenue — HIGH |
| Owner works 50+ hrs | ⚠️ Owner-dependent operations — MEDIUM |
| No #2 / manager | ⚠️ Key person risk — MEDIUM |
| Lease < 24 months | ⚠️ Lease risk — HIGH |
| Revenue declining | ⚠️ Revenue trend — HIGH |
| Tax returns only | ⚠️ Financial verifiability — MEDIUM |
| Cash basis accounting | ⚠️ Accrual adjustments needed — MEDIUM |
| Spouse works, won't stay | ⚠️ Replacement cost — MEDIUM |
| No contracts | ⚠️ Revenue not contracted — MEDIUM |
| Seasonal > 50% one quarter | ⚠️ Seasonality risk — MEDIUM |
| Family on payroll | ⚠️ Add-back scrutiny — LOW |

## RISK NARRATIVES
Write narratives that are HONEST, not polished.
Structure:
1. State the risk plainly
2. State what IS in place to mitigate
3. State what IS NOT in place
4. Recommend buyer action
