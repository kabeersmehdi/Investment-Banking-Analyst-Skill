# Web Research Protocol v3.0

## WHEN TO SEARCH
Search automatically when ANY command is run.
Search queries are derived from DEAL CONTEXT fields.

| Command | What to Search |
|---------|---------------|
| /valuation | "[industry] M&A multiples 2024 2025", "[industry] SDE multiple lower middle market", "BizBuySell [industry] median sale price" |
| /cim | "[industry] market size US 2025", "[industry] CAGR", "[industry] industry trends" |
| /buyers | "who is acquiring [industry] companies 2024 2025", "[industry] PE acquisitions", "[industry] strategic acquirers" |
| /risks | "[industry] risks 2025", "[industry] regulatory changes", "[industry] labor market" |
| /structure | "current SBA 7a interest rate 2025", "SBA 7a maximum loan 2025" |
| /qoe | "[industry] average EBITDA margin", "[industry] gross margin benchmark" |

### Tier 1 (Use First)
BizBuySell Insight Reports, IBBA Market Pulse, Axial,
DealStats, GF Data, IBISWorld

### Tier 2 (Good Supplement)
Divestopedia, industry trade publications, SEC filings,
Mergr, DealRoom

### Tier 3 (Use With Caution)
Statista, Grand View Research, general news articles,
blog posts
