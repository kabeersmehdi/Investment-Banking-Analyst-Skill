## DEAL CERTAINTY SCORE (1-10 each)
- Financing pre-approved?
- Buyer has prior acquisitions?
- Diligence < 45 days?
- Few contingencies?
- Legal counsel engaged?
- Earnout < 15% of total?

## RED FLAGS
⚠️ "Subject to financing" with no pre-approval
⚠️ Diligence > 60 days
⚠️ Earnout > 25% of consideration
⚠️ Retrading language
⚠️ No deposit
⚠️ Exclusivity > 90 days
