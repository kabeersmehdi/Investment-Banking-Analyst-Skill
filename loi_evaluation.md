## LOI Evaluation Framework

### Offer Comparison Matrix
| Factor | Weight | Offer A | Offer B | Offer C |
|--------|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Total Consideration | 25% | $X | $X | $X |

### Deal Certainty Scoring (1–10)
| Factor | Score | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Financing pre-approval | /10 | Pre-approved SBA > "seeking financing" |
| Buyer experience | /10 | Prior acquisitions > first-time |
| Diligence timeline | /10 | 30–45 days > 90+ days |
| Contingencies | /10 | Fewer = better |
| Legal counsel engaged | /10 | Engaged > "will hire" |
| Earnout % of total | /10 | Lower % = more certain value |
| **Total Certainty Score** | **/60** | |

### LOI Red Flags
⚠️ "Subject to financing" with no pre-approval letter
⚠️ Diligence period > 60 days
⚠️ Earnout > 25% of total consideration
⚠️ Broad retrading language ("price subject to diligence findings")
⚠️ No deposit / escrow at LOI
⚠️ Buyer has no legal counsel identified
⚠️ Exclusivity period > 90 days
