# Engagement Guidance v3.0

## Fee Structures (LMM Standard)

### Success Fee
| Enterprise Value | Fee |
|-----------------|-----|
| First $1M | 10-12% |
| $1M-$2M | 8-10% |
| $2M-$5M | 6-8% |
| $5M-$10M | 4-6% |

### Minimum Fee
Typical: $75K-$150K

### Monthly Retainer
$2K-$5K/month, credited against success fee.

### Engagement Term
Exclusive: 12 months | Tail: 24 months

### Double Lehman (Sub-$2M Deals)
First $1M: 10% | Next $1M: 8% | Thereafter: 6%
