## Standard LMM Broker Fee Structures

### Success Fee (Lehman-Based)
| Enterprise Value | Typical Fee |
|-----------------|-------------|
| First $1M | 10–12% |
| $1M–$2M | 8–10% |
| $2M–$5M | 6–8% |
| $5M–$10M | 4–6% |

### Minimum Fee
Most LMM brokers set a minimum of $75K–$150K.

### Retainer
- Monthly retainer: $2K–$5K/month
- Credited against success fee at close
- Purpose: Filter serious sellers from tire-kickers

### Engagement Term
- Exclusive listing: 12 months typical
- Tail period: 24 months (protects broker if buyer surfaces post-term)

### Double Lehman (Common for Sub-$2M Deals)
| Enterprise Value | Fee |
|-----------------|-----|
| First $1M | 10% |
| Next $1M | 8% |
| Thereafter | 6% |
